About Us

The Laurel Review is a biannual print magazine seeking submissions of poetry, fiction, creative nonfiction, script, review, and
those that blur the lines between genres. We seek both established and new voices in the writing world, and we are
especially interested in writing by those whose identities have been historically under-represented. Submitters living outside
of the United States are encouraged to submit. Translations in various genres are also of interest. All accepted submitters will
receive two copies of the issue in which their piece(s) appear/s.
